Reconstruction Functions
========================

Here is the list of functions to reconstruct various properties on the representation grid.

.. list-table::
	
    * - :doc:`reconstruct_AO<reconstruction/reconstruct_AO>`
      - reconstructs the AO
    * - :doc:`reconstruct_MO<reconstruction/reconstruct_MO>`
      - reconstructs the MO
    * - :doc:`reconstruct_chosen_MO<reconstruction/reconstruct_chosen_MO>`
      - reconstructs a chosen MO
    * - :doc:`reconstruct_electron_density<reconstruction/reconstruct_electron_density>`
      - reconstructs the electron density
    * - :doc:`rreconstruct_fukui_function<reconstruction/reconstruct_fukui_function>`
      - reconstructs the fukui function
    * - :doc:`reconstruct_transition_density<reconstruction/reconstruct_transition_density>`
      - reconstructs the transition densities
    * - :doc:`reconstruct_chosen_transition_density<reconstruction/reconstruct_chosen_transition_density>`
      - reconstructs a chosen transition density
    * - :doc:`reconstruct_eigenvectors<reconstruction/reconstruct_eigenvectors>`
      - reconstructs the eigenvectors of a kernel
